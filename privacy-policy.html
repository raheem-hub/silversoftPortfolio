GDPR Data Protection Rights

  •  The right of information- to know what personal data is collected and by whom.
  •  The right of access- to submit access requests and attain information about whether their personal information is being processed.
  •  The right to rectification- - to update any inaccurate or incomplete data that had been obtained from the user.
  •  The right to erasure- to ask for your personal data to be deleted under certain conditions.
  •  The right to restrict processing- to request and limit how personal data is used, although We are not automatically required to delete it.
  •  The right to data portability- to obtain personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  •  The right to object to processing, under lawful bases.
